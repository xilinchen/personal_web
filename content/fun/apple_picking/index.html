I recently went to apple picking on a sunny weekend. Fall in Michigan especially around this time is very beautiful. Going to apple picking is a nice activity to enjoy the fall foliage in the country and get some workout by working and picking the apples.

The apple pick farm I went was pretty nice. I loved their warm donuts, despite there was a wait. The minimum apples to purchase was to fill a half bushel! It is A LOT apples!

Here are some things I did/plan to do:

  • Eat an apple a day for a week during lunch
  • Bring a bag to work to share with coworkers
  • Give a bag to the neighbor
  • Cook them!
